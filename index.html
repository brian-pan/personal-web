Dish Guru

An self-built full-stack application with MongoDB,Express, React, and NodeJS (MERN). Users are able to explore various recipes created by other users and write their reviews for these recipes. An user can view, edit, and write their own recipes after register/login. All data including users, dish recipes, and their associated reviews are processed and passed using Redux and Express, then stored in MongoDB.
